Written by 12 people in a dhaba at 2am. Peer-reviewed by the chai wallah.

Relatives who ask "When are you getting married?" at family functions must pay a cess of ₹500 per question. Revenue funds mental health services.

09

Trains will run on time. Or at least within a 4-hour window. This is our most ambitious policy and we are terrified.

10

Jugaad As Heritage

Jugaad will be declared intangible cultural heritage. The bamboo + rubber band engineering tradition of India deserves UNESCO recognition. We've applied.
